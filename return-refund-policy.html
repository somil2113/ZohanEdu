Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: February 2025

At ZOHANEDU, we want you to be satisfied with your learning experience. This policy outlines the conditions under which you may request a refund for course purchases.

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee: If you are not satisfied with a course within 30 days of purchase, you may request a full refund, provided you have not completed more than 20% of the course content.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

You may be eligible for a refund if:

  • Your request is made within 30 days of the purchase date
  • You have consumed less than 20% of the course content (videos, modules, or equivalent)
  • The course is not part of a bundle or promotion with different refund terms

2. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be granted in the following cases:

  • More than 30 days have passed since purchase
  • You have completed more than 20% of the course
  • You have already received a certificate of completion for the course
  • Abuse or violation of our Terms & Conditions (e.g., sharing access, chargebacks)

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  • Email us at zhnttt.info@gmail.com with the subject "Refund Request"
  • Include your registered email, course name, and order/payment reference
  • Briefly state the reason for your request

We will respond within 5–7 business days. Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 10–14 business days.

4. Course Access After Refund

Once a refund is processed, your access to the course will be revoked. You may re-enroll at any time by purchasing again.

5. Exceptions

For subscription plans or bundled offerings, specific refund terms will be communicated at the time of purchase. In case of technical issues that prevent access to paid content, we will work to resolve the issue or offer a refund at our discretion.

6. Contact Us

For refund requests or questions about this policy, please contact us.
